How to Submit a Great Request

Before hitting submit, take 10 minutes to think through your idea properly. A well-documented request gets built faster and gets built right.

1
Talk it through with AI first
Open ChatGPT or Claude and describe your idea out loud. Ask it to challenge your thinking, poke holes, and help you get specific. "I want X because Y and the outcome I expect is Z."
2
Answer the key questions
What do you want built or changed? Why does it matter to your business? Who is it for? What does success look like? Any examples or references you love?
3
Write it up in a Word doc
Doesn't need to be pretty — just organized. A short summary at the top, then the detail. Screenshots or links are helpful if you have them.
4
Upload & submit
Drag the doc into the upload zone below, pick a request type, add any quick notes, and hit Send. Aiden will read your doc, assess what's possible, and respond with next steps.
Pro tip: The more specific you are, the faster we can build it. Vague requests take longer because we have to ask follow-up questions. Specific requests ship faster.
