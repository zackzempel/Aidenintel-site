How to Submit a Great Request

Before hitting submit, take 10 minutes to think through your idea properly. A well-documented request gets built faster and gets built right.

1
Talk it through with Aiden
Click 💬 Talk to Aiden and describe your idea. Aiden will ask the right questions, shape it into a clear request, and generate the document for you automatically. Then just review and hit Submit — done.
2
OR write it up yourself
Already know exactly what you want? Write a short doc — what you want, why it matters, and what success looks like. Screenshots or links are helpful.
3
Upload & submit
Drag your doc into the upload zone, pick a category from the dropdown, and hit Send. Aiden will review it and respond with next steps.
Pro tip: The more specific you are, the faster we can build it. Vague requests take longer because we have to ask follow-up questions. Specific requests ship faster.
