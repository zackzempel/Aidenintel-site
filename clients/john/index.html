A Few Things We Noticed
⚠️
The "Schedule a Visit" CTA is hard to find on mobile
Your most important conversion action — getting a parent to book a tour — is buried below the fold on smaller screens. Parents are researching daycares on their phones during nap time. If they have to scroll to find the button, many won't. This is likely costing you leads every day.
🔴
No visible follow-up after a visit request
When a parent fills out your contact form, what happens next? If the answer is "someone checks email and replies eventually," you're losing families to competitors who follow up within minutes. Speed to response is one of the biggest factors in daycare enrollment decisions.
💡
Multi-location coordination is an untapped asset
Having locations in both Illinois and Minnesota is a strength, but the site treats them almost identically. Location-specific landing pages with local SEO, community ties, and tailored messaging could meaningfully increase organic traffic at each site.
