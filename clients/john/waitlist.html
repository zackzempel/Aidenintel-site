⚡ How Automated Waitlist Outreach Works
When a spot opens up, Aiden handles the outreach automatically — so your team never has to manually chase down families or let a spot go unfilled.
Step 1
Spot Opens Up
An enrolled family cancels or moves up. The system detects the opening instantly.
Step 2
Top Family Gets a Message
The highest-priority family gets a personalized text + email within 60 seconds. Warm, on-brand, from Rayito de Sol.
Step 3
Tour Booked or Next Family Notified
If they respond, a tour is booked automatically. If not within 24hrs, the next family is contacted.
