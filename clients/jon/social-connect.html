🤖 Automated Pipeline

Your Social Media
Runs Itself

Submit a listing once. Aiden generates platform-perfect captions and posts your video to Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and YouTube — automatically, every time.

How It Works
1

You submit a listing

Drop the listing address and AGNT Media video URL into your portal. That's the only thing you ever do.

2

Aiden writes the captions Auto

Platform-optimized captions are generated for each channel — Instagram hooks + hashtags, punchy TikTok copy, conversational Facebook, SEO-rich YouTube descriptions. All tailored to real estate and your brand.

3

Your video posts to all 4 platforms Auto

Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and YouTube get the video and caption simultaneously. No copy-pasting, no logging into multiple apps, no forgetting a platform.

4

You get a confirmation text

A quick Telegram notification confirms everything went through. You can verify the posts or just move on — they're already live.
